How it works

Three steps to AI fluency.

Agent Passport runs locally in your browser. No cloud. No accounts required.

Step One
🧲

Capture

As you chat with any AI, Agent Passport silently watches and extracts facts about you — your name, role, preferences, projects, decisions, and constraints. All heuristically, all locally.

Step Two
🔍

Review

Nothing gets saved without your approval. Review extracted memories in your Inbox. Approve, edit, or reject each one. You are always in control of your personal context.

Step Three
⚡

Inject

Open any AI platform and click Inject — your entire personal context lands in the input field, perfectly formatted. The AI knows you from sentence one. Enable auto-inject to never think about it again.
