01

.agentpassport

An open file format for portable AI context. Drop it in any project, version it in git, share it. Like .env but for who you are.

02

Runtime

A local service that manages your passport and controls access. Any AI client — browser, CLI, IDE, desktop — connects to it.

03

Clients Everywhere

Chrome extension. VS Code. CLI. SDK. The runtime speaks to every AI platform you use. One passport, everywhere.

How it works

1

Capture

Your conversations reveal who you are. Agent Passport extracts facts — name, preferences, stack, decisions — as candidates.

2

Review

You approve, edit, or reject every memory. Nothing is saved without explicit consent. Your data, your rules.

3

Inject

One command sends your approved context to any AI. Every conversation starts with full context, instantly.
