The Solution

Your passport for
the agent internet

Agent Passport captures what matters about you, travels across every AI platform, and injects your context automatically.

01
🧠 Intelligent Memory
Automatically extract and organize memories from your conversations — skills, preferences, projects, goals — into a structured knowledge graph.
02
🛂 Portable Passport
Your identity document for the AI era. A structured, versioned, cryptographically-owned record of who you are and what you need from AI.
03
🔐 Permission Grants
Granular control over what each AI platform can know about you. Grant, revoke, and audit access with a single click.
04
🚀 Instant Injection
One-click context injection into ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini and Grok. AI already knows you before you type a word.
05
🏠 Local-first Privacy
Your data never leaves your device. No cloud sync, no telemetry, no third-party servers. Fully open source and auditable.
06
🔌 Open Standard
Agent Passport is built on open schemas. Any AI tool can integrate it. Any developer can extend it. The future of AI identity is open.

How It Works

Ready in 60 seconds

1
Install the Chrome extension
Add Agent Passport to Chrome in one click. Works on Mac, Windows and Linux.
2
Build your passport
Add memories manually or let the extension extract them from your existing AI conversations automatically.
3
Grant permissions
Choose exactly what each AI platform is allowed to know about you. Full control, always.
4
Inject & go
Open any supported AI platform and click Inject. Your context is loaded instantly. Every AI already knows you.
